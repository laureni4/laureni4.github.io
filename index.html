Welcome to the Neighborhood

Ever had a craving for good old-fashioned ice cream? Are your local ice cream stores selling too many weird flavors that make your taste buds shrink at the thought of them? At Steve's, we believe in the classics, and put all of our efforts into making the best homemade ice cream you've ever tasted!

We operate mainly out of our truck, which circles around the Cambridge area, between Harvard University and MIT. See our route to see where we stop.

In addition to our truck, we have a brand-new physical location in Independence, MA. Although we used to make all of our ice cream on the truck, this is now where all production happens. New batches are sent out to Cambridge daily.
