Route

In addition to our store location at 1776 Americas Avenue, our trucks travel across Cambridge, bringing the goods to you! Circling between Harvard University, MIT, and Porter Square, we serve a large populus, and are looking to expand as well. We have several designated stops on our route:

  1. Harvard University, 12:00-1:00, 6:15-8:00
  2. Central Square, 1:15-2:00, 8:15-9:00
  3. Portland St./Binney St., 2:15-3:00
  4. Somerville Ave/Prospect St., 3:15-4:00
  5. Somerville Ave/Lowell St., 4:15-6:00

If you live within a one mile radius of our route, we deliver as well. Tel: 555-5555
